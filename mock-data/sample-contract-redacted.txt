MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT
Between: Acme Cloud Technologies Inc. ("Vendor") and Customer ("Enterprise")

SECTION 1 — SERVICES
Vendor shall provide cloud-based data processing services as described
in applicable Order Forms. Vendor may modify or discontinue any feature
with 7 days written notice.

SECTION 2 — PAYMENT TERMS
Customer shall pay all invoices within 15 days. Late payments accrue
interest at 2% per month. Vendor may suspend services for any payment
5 days overdue without liability. All fees are non-refundable.

SECTION 3 — DATA & PRIVACY
Customer grants Vendor a perpetual, irrevocable licence to use
anonymised customer data for product improvement. Vendor may share data
with third-party sub-processors at its discretion without prior notice.
Customer data deleted within 365 days of contract termination.

SECTION 4 — INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All work product created by Vendor using Customer requirements shall
be solely owned by Vendor. Customer receives a non-exclusive licence
during the term only.

SECTION 5 — LIABILITY
Vendor's total liability shall not exceed fees paid in the prior
30 days. Vendor not liable for any indirect or consequential damages.
Customer waives all class action rights.

SECTION 6 — INDEMNIFICATION
Customer shall indemnify Vendor against all third-party claims.
Vendor's indemnification limited to IP infringement only, capped at
$10,000.

SECTION 7 — SERVICE LEVELS
Vendor targets 99.0% monthly uptime but provides no warranty.
SLA credits limited to 5% of monthly fees. Planned maintenance
excluded with no minimum notice.

SECTION 8 — TERMINATION
Auto-renews for 12-month terms unless cancelled with 90 days notice.
Customer has 30 days post-termination to export data.

SECTION 9 — GOVERNING LAW
Governed by laws of the Cayman Islands. All disputes by binding
arbitration in the Cayman Islands. Jury trial waived.
